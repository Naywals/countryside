Pedestrian Sign.

Hiking signage:This rustic wooden sign is a directional marker. The white pictogram depicts a hiker with a backpack and walking stick. its function:It serves as a "silent guide." It points to the right, confirming to the hiker that she is on the right path to reach a marked trail. It is a symbol of safety and organized adventure.

tiered Waterfall

It's a waterfall :we can see The water doesn't fall in one go, but slides over several levels of limestone rock. This creates a very aesthetically pleasing curtain of white, foaming water. The pool: At the foot of the falls, the water gathers in a large pool. The color of the water varies from emerald green to light blue, indicating very pure water, often rich in minerals.

The Farmyard: This is where hens, roosters, chicks, ducks, geese, turkeys, and often rabbits live.
